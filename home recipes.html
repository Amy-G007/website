Bread

  • 8 - heaping cups of flour
  • 1 - heaping mixing spoon of sugar
  • less than 1 level mixing spoon of salt
  • 3 - heaping Tblsp of Yeast
  • 4 cups - hot water
  • 2/3 cups - vegetable oil
  • 3 - eggs
  • canned milk

Put all the flour in a big bowl, create a well, add sugar, salt, and yeast - mix in with a bit of the flour.
Add 3/4 of the hot water, pour in oil and mix slightly.
In a 1 cup measuring cup add the 3 eggs and fill to the 1-cup line with canned milk, whisk until combined, and pour into bowl
Slowly incorporate the flour to the water, halfway through add the rest of the hot water. knead for 10-12 minutes until dough doesn't stick to your hands.
Cover with towel and let rise for about an hour, punch down, let rise once more for another hour.
Spray or lard 6 loaf pans, set oven to 360 degrees, use a bread knife and divide dough into 6 pieces. knead bubbles out, roll into loaf shape, roll in pan then cover with towel and let rise until it doubles in size.
Bake for 30 minutes

Mom's Best Spice Cake

  • 3/4 cups - Butter
  • 1 1/2 cups - Sugar
  • 3 - Egg yolks, beated
  • 1 tsp - Vanilla
  • 1 cup - Milk
  • 1/2 tsp - Salt
  • 2 tsp - Baking Powder
  • 1/2 tsp - All Spice
  • 1/4 tsp - Cloves
  • 1 tsp - Cinnamon
  • 2 1/4 cups - Flour
  • 1 cup - raisins
  • 2/3 cups - chopped nuts
  • 3 - Egg Whites

    • Cream butter, add sugar gradually, beating until fluffy
    Add beaten egg yolks, vanilla, and milk, beat until smooth.
    Stir salt, baking powder, spices, and flour together in a small bowl, add nuts and raisins.
    Combine flour mixture into batter. Beat egg whites until stiff but not dry, fold gently into batter.
    Grease and flour loaf pan, bake at 350 degrees for 50-60 minutes

Rich and Flaky Pie Crust

  • 2 cups - Flour
  • 2 tblsp - Sugar
  • 1/2 tsp- Salt
  • 1/4 tsp - Baking Powder
  • 1/2 cup - Butter
  • 1/4 cup - Lard
  • 6-10 tblsp - Ice Cold Water

    • stir flour, sugar, salt, and baking powder together. Cut in lard and butter with 2 knives until mixture is crumbly.
    Add water a little at a time until you can form a ball with the dough. Handle as little as possible
    bake at 350 degrees for 50 minutes

Everything Cookies

  • 1 cup - Hard Margarine (or butter), Softened
  • 2 cups - Brown Sugar, Packed
  • 2 - Large Eggs
  • 1 tsp - Vanilla Extract
  • 2 cups - All-purpose Flour
  • 1 tsp - Baking Powder
  • 1/2 tsp - Baking Soda
  • 1/4 tsp - Nutmeg
  • 1/4 tsp - Allspice
  • 1/4 tsp - Cloves
  • 2 cups - Quick-cooking Rolled Oats
  • 2 cups - chocolate chips
  • 1/2 cup - coconuts
  • 1/2 cup - Chopped Walnuts

    • Cream butter and brown sugar in large bowl. Add eggs 1 at a time, beating well after each addition. Add vanilla. Beat until smooth
    Combine next 6 ingredients in a small bowl. Add to butter mixture in 2 additions, mixing well after each addition until no dry flour remains
    Adding remaining ingredients. Mix well. Drop, using 1 tbsp for each, about 2 inches apart on a greased cookie sheet. Bake in 350 degree oven for 13 minutes until golden. Let stand on cookie sheet for 5 minutes. Remove from cookie sheet and place on a wire rack to cool.
